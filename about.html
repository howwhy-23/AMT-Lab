05

Challenges Faced

First issue: Slider Generates Useless Noises. When I was developing Wath, I find there will generate some Harsh and Loud sound. I fix it continuous for 3 hours, but still having no solution. Luckily, after checking numerous of articles, I found the issue called UnFull Cycling issue, means the sound wave isn't perfectly, equally and symmetry generate by the coding logic, when I fixed it, the whole function is perfect and well done.

Second issue: Cross-origin isolation. It is a safety policy that set by Google, it's critical problem is even though I has ensure the ffmpeg's JS and WASM is correct, it still can't load, and it will let the FFmuvisual can't export video. This issue is fatal but it's solution is very simple, which is letting the URL of the webside be "localhost" or "http" or "https", every problem about this issue made have been cracked when I use http URL.

Third issue, is also the most complex and severe issue: not recording the Canvas in the FFmuvisual properly. It seems like a easy problem right? But it is the hardest challenge in this whole webpage. I tried rewrite the drawing canvas function, but it doesn't solve any single problem, then I tried to troubleshoot the problem in the FFmpeg recording function, also failed. I once collapsed, asking me myself again and again, is it possible to do that? But I still not totally giving up, I found more and more articles that relates to this problem. Finally! After finding about 80+ times, I found the critical point, it is: let FFmpeg record three section, which is background, canvas and element. Then, running it in order: 1. Background , 2. Canvas , 3. Element, this is the order of their z-index. Last, ensure all of the JS file were working well in console. Then...., problem fixed!
